Recruitment Process

1

Interest Registration

Recruiters express interest via our online portal or direct contact with the placement cell. Initial data exchange occurs.

2

Skill Assessment

Companies define specific technical and soft skill requirements. We shortlist candidates based on academic performance and project portfolios.

3

Interview Coordination

Seamless scheduling of on-campus or virtual interviews, GDs, and technical rounds facilitated by our logistics team.

4

Final Selection

Consolidation of results and issuance of offer letters. Onboarding support provided for successful candidates.
