Vision

The school’s published vision is already strong and specific.

SSE describes its aim as developing dynamic and socially responsible engineers possessing wisdom, positive attitude, and impeccable character, with innovation, initiative, teamwork, and responsiveness to change as hallmarks.

  • Dynamic and socially responsible engineers
  • Wisdom, positive attitude, and impeccable character
  • Innovation, initiative, and teamwork
  • Ability to anticipate change and create opportunity

Mission

The mission statement centers on quality, skills, and service.

Quality education

The college states that it serves society and the nation through quality education and skill development.

Research and innovation

It commits to new benchmarks in engineering education through research, development, and innovation.

Service orientation

The mission explicitly references service to society, industry, and the wider world.
