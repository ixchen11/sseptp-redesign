Ask admissions

Before families decide

The institution supports guidance here, but not a fully reliable public fee table.

This page helps families understand what to clarify where public fee details need confirmation.

01

Programme fee clarity

Confirm tuition expectations for the chosen route instead of assuming one flat cost structure across all programmes.

02

Additional cost heads

Families usually also need clarity on hostel, transport, examination, and other joining-related charges where relevant.

03

Scholarship interaction

Ask directly how scholarship support may reduce or influence the payable fee burden in the student’s case.

What to ask admissions

A useful fees page reduces uncertainty even when it avoids unsupported public numbers.

This page gives families the right questions so they can reach a confident decision quickly.

A

Tuition

What is the current tuition for the selected programme and academic year?

B

Residential costs

If hostel accommodation is needed, what are the expected boarding and lodging charges?

C

Other charges

Are there transport, examination, or one-time admission-related costs families should plan for?

D

Payment timing

How are payment milestones structured, and what changes if scholarship support is approved?
