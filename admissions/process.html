Step-by-step

This page presents a clearer admissions sequence.

That practical order stays visible here so applicants understand both the process and the checkpoints.

01

Eligibility check

Confirm that you meet the minimum academic criteria for the programme, especially PCM-based requirements for B.Tech admission.

02

Application

Complete the online application and prepare supporting documents in advance instead of treating them as a later step.

03

Entrance examination

Appear for the relevant state-level or national engineering entrance examination route referenced by the institution.

04

Counselling

Participate in counselling and seat-allocation stages based on merit and the applicable admission framework.

05

Admission confirmation

Verify documents, confirm fees, and complete final formalities to secure the seat.

Basic eligibility

Applicants can now see the core requirement set without hunting for it.

The institution already provides usable admissions criteria for B.Tech pathways and general entrance logic.

B.Tech baseline

10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics

Minimum 50% aggregate in PCM (45% for reserved categories)

Valid entrance-exam route as applicable

Age note: institution mentions a not-more-than-23 reference for the 2025 cycle

Required documents

Document readiness is part of the process, not an afterthought.

The institution names a practical document set that is summarized here for applicants and families.

A

Academic records

10th and 12th mark sheets / certificates, plus equivalent qualification records where relevant.

B

Admission records

Transfer Certificate and the relevant entrance-exam score card should be prepared early.

C

Identity & support records

Aadhar Card, passport-size photographs, and category certificate where applicable.
