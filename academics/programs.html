Programme detail

Each programme includes enough detail for serious comparison.

The structure moves from a scan-first overview into curriculum rhythm, labs, careers, and outcome signals.

Computer Science & Engineering

The current page positions CSE around software systems, data structures, algorithms, databases, networking, artificial intelligence, and machine learning.

  • Course structure: First year foundations, then data structures / DBMS / OS, then software engineering / networks / AI / ML / cloud, then distributed systems / cybersecurity / big data / IoT.
  • Lab facilities: Advanced Computing Lab, Network & Security Lab, Database Systems Lab, Software Development Lab, AI & ML Lab, IoT Lab.
  • Career routes: Software Developer, Systems Analyst, DBA, Network Engineer, Web / Mobile Developer, AI/ML Engineer, Data Scientist.
  • Achievement signal: national coding competitions, hackathons, and placements into firms like TCS, Infosys, Wipro, and startups.

Electronics & Communication Engineering

ECE is described through electronic systems, circuits, communication networks, VLSI, embedded systems, signal processing, and wireless communication.

  • Course structure: electronics and communication foundations, then electronic devices / digital systems / analog circuits, then DSP / VLSI / microcontrollers / control / wireless, then embedded, optical, satellite, and IoT systems.
  • Lab facilities: Basic Electronics Lab, Communication Systems Lab, VLSI Design Lab, Microprocessor & Microcontroller Lab, DSP Lab, IoT & Embedded Systems Lab.
  • Career routes: Electronics Design Engineer, Communication Systems Engineer, VLSI Design Engineer, IoT Developer, Network Planning Engineer, Embedded Systems Designer.
  • Achievement signal: technical-competition wins and placements into electronics, telecom, and PSU environments.

Electrical & Electronics Engineering

EEE is framed around generation, transmission, distribution, and utilization of electrical energy, with power systems and renewable energy as strong themes.

  • Course structure: electric circuits and machines, then power systems / control / power electronics / microcontrollers / drives, then high-voltage engineering, smart grids, protection, and renewable-energy systems.
  • Lab facilities: Electrical Machines Lab, Power Systems Lab, Control Systems Lab, Power Electronics Lab, Electrical Measurements Lab, Microprocessor & Microcontroller Lab.
  • Career routes: Electrical Design Engineer, Power Systems Engineer, Control Systems Engineer, Renewable Energy Specialist, Automation Engineer.
  • Achievement signal: technical-festival recognition and placements into power, manufacturing, and automation companies.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering is presented through design, manufacturing, maintenance, thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, materials science, CAD/CAM, and robotics.

  • Course structure: strong first-year basics, then mechanics / thermodynamics / materials / manufacturing, then heat transfer / design / hydraulics / CAD-CAM, then robotics, industrial engineering, automotive, HVAC, and project work.
  • Lab facilities: Engineering Mechanics Lab, Thermal Engineering Lab, Fluid Mechanics Lab, Manufacturing Technology Lab, CAD/CAM Lab, Robotics Lab.
  • Career routes: Mechanical Design Engineer, Production Engineer, Quality Control Engineer, Automotive Engineer, HVAC Engineer, CAD/CAM Specialist, Robotics Engineer.
  • Achievement signal: SAE BAJA, Go-Kart, and related design challenges, plus placements into manufacturing and automotive firms.

Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering is described through buildings, roads, bridges, water systems, structural design, geotechnics, transportation, environmental engineering, and construction management.

  • Course structure: surveying / strength of materials / fluids / soil mechanics, then reinforced concrete / steel / highways / foundations / water resources / environmental engineering, then advanced design and construction-management topics.
  • Lab facilities: Material Testing Lab, Surveying Lab, Structural Engineering Lab, Geotechnical Engineering Lab, Environmental Engineering Lab, CAD Lab.
  • Career routes: Structural Engineer, Construction Manager, Geotechnical Engineer, Transportation Engineer, Environmental Engineer, Urban Planner, Site Engineer.
  • Achievement signal: national design competitions and placements into construction, consulting, and government organisations.
